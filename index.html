Jianyuan Ni

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Texas State University (TXST), where I am co-advised by Prof. Yan Yan and Prof. Anne H.H. Ngu. Currently, I am also working as an Intern researcher under the supervision of Dr. Hao Tang at ETH Zurich, Switzerland. Before TXST, I recieved my M.S. degrees from Lamar University under the supervision of Prof. Jing Zhang.

My research focuses on how to leverage Multimodal Learning to improve Sensor-based Human Activity Recognition (HAR) performance. Specifically, My research goal is to advance wearable deviece HAR field from the perspective of Crossmodal Learning. Currently, I serve as reviewer for multiple international conferences and journals such as ACM MM, ICASSP, NeuroComputing, TMM and etc.
